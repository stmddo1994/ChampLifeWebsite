Headings

HTML provides six levels of headings, from <h1> (most important) to <h6> (least important).

<h1>Main Heading</h1> <h2>Subheading</h2> <h3>Sub-subheading</h3>

Paragraphs and Text

Paragraphs are created with the <p> tag. Bold text uses <strong> and italic text uses <em>.

You can also use <b> for bold and <i> for italic, but <strong> and <em> are semantically better.
